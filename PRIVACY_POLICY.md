# Juno Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2025  
**App Name:** Juno – Period & Women's Health Tracker  
**Package:** com.juno.tracker

---

## The Short Version

Juno collects zero data about you. Your data never leaves your phone. There are no servers, no accounts, no analytics. Period.

---

## What Data Juno Stores

Juno stores the following data **locally on your device only**:

- Your name and birth year (optional, for personalization)
- Period start dates and cycle history
- Daily log entries (flow, symptoms, mood, sleep, temperature, weight, notes)
- App settings and preferences
- Article bookmarks

All data is stored in a local SQLite database on your device using Expo SQLite. This database is never transmitted, synced, or uploaded anywhere.

---

## What Data Juno Does NOT Collect

- ❌ No account registration or login
- ❌ No email address, phone number, or identifying information
- ❌ No analytics or usage statistics
- ❌ No crash reporting (no Sentry, Crashlytics, etc.)
- ❌ No advertising identifiers
- ❌ No location data
- ❌ No third-party tracking SDKs
- ❌ No cloud sync
- ❌ No internet connection required or used at runtime

---

## Notifications

Juno uses **local notifications only** — scheduled directly on your device by the app. No notification server or push service is used. Notifications are entirely processed on-device.

---

## Biometrics

If you enable biometric authentication (fingerprint / face unlock), Juno uses your device's built-in biometric system. Biometric data never leaves your device and is never accessible to the app — we only receive a pass/fail result from the operating system.

---

## Backup & Export

When you export a backup:

1. Juno creates a JSON file on your device's local storage
2. You choose where to save it via your device's share sheet
3. No copy is made by Juno — you are in complete control of the file

Juno has no access to any backups you save to external services (Google Drive, etc.).

---

## Children

Juno is intended for users aged 13 and older. Juno does not knowingly collect any data from anyone.

---

## Changes to This Policy

If the app is updated to change how data is handled, the new policy will be displayed in the app before any changes take effect.

---

## Contact

Since Juno has no servers and no accounts, there is no way to contact us through the app. If you have questions, open an issue in the project's public repository.

---

**Your body data belongs to you. Always.**
